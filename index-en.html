What is “cryptomania”?

Price explosions, media hype, promises of instant wealth… but underneath the noise, there is a genuine technological revolution.

💰 Cryptocurrency

100% digital money, secured by cryptography and managed by a distributed network. No bank branch, no central bank; just code and nodes spread all over the planet.

⛓️ Blockchain

A shared, append-only ledger. Each block contains transactions and is linked to the previous one via a hash. Changing the past becomes practically impossible.

🚀 Hype & speculation

FOMO, bull runs, crashes: the cycles repeat. Understanding the mechanics helps you avoid being swept away by euphoria or panic.
